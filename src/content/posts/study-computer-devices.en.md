In an increasingly digitally connected world, choosing the right technology devices is a crucial step toward meeting our diverse needs. This module guides you through understanding the characteristics and buying guidelines for various types of devices, from the simplest to the most advanced.

## Choosing a Device: Characteristics and Considerations

When selecting technology devices, there is no *one-size-fits-all* solution. A device's characteristics must align with each user's needs and preferences.

| Aspect | Key Question |
|---|---|
| **Performance** | Do you need a high-performance device for heavy tasks, or will a standard one do? |
| **Mobility** | Is portability important, or will the device stay in one location? |
| **Screen Quality & Resolution** | How critical is screen sharpness and size — especially for laptops and tablets? |
| **Price & Budget** | What budget do you have, and how do you balance it against features? |

## Types of Devices


![Peta kategori perangkat teknologi konsumen dan karakteristik utamanya](/images/inline/study-computer-devices-1.svg)

The range of devices to be covered:

- **Laptop** — portable and versatile, ideal for users on the move.
- **Tablet** — a blend of smartphone and laptop, equipped with a touchscreen.
- **Desktop** — a stationary, powerful computer for heavy workloads.
- **Smartphone** — a smart device for communication and a wide range of digital activities.
- **Digital Camera** — captures moments with high image quality.
- **Portable Media Player** — stores and plays music and videos.
- **Digital Media Player** — *streams* and stores digital content.

## Specialized Technology

### Servers, Supercomputers, and POS Terminals

- **Server** — used for data storage and management.
- **Supercomputer** — the world's most powerful data-processing machines.
- **Point-of-Sale (POS) Terminal** — used in retail businesses to record transactions.

### Self-Service Kiosks and ATMs

Self-service kiosks and ATMs are revolutionizing the modern shopping experience and banking services, bringing automation that speeds up everyday transactions.

### E-book Readers and Wearable Devices

For book lovers, e-book readers let you carry an entire library wherever you go. Meanwhile, *wearable* technology such as smartwatches and fitness trackers has become an integral part of the digital lifestyle.

### Gaming Devices

For gamers, the options are diverse — from consoles to dedicated gaming PCs, each with its own strengths.

## Embedded Computers and Cloud Computing


![Spektrum komputasi dari perangkat tertanam hingga komputasi awan](/images/inline/study-computer-devices-2.svg)

In the digital era, computers don't only come in the form of devices we hold in our hands.

> **Embedded computers** are hidden inside various everyday appliances — from washing machines to cars. Meanwhile, **cloud computing** enables access to massive computing resources over the internet.

## Ports, Connections, and Maintenance

### Ports and Connections

Ports and connection types are important technical aspects that determine a device's compatibility and flexibility. Recognizing the different port variations helps you ensure your device can connect to the accessories and peripherals you need.

### Device Maintenance

Choosing the right device alone is not enough — proper care is key to extending its lifespan and keeping it efficient. You will learn how to properly protect and maintain your *hardware*.
