Computers have become an inseparable part of daily life. Module 1 introduces the basic concepts of computers and outlines their roles and types across a variety of situations.

## Types of Computers

Computers come in many forms, from the simple to the sophisticated, each designed for specific needs.

| Device | Key Characteristics |
| --- | --- |
| **Laptop** | Versatile and portable, ideal for mobility. |
| **Tablet** | A blend of smartphone and laptop with a touchscreen. |
| **Desktop** | A stationary computer for the office or home. |
| **Server** | A dedicated computer for storing and managing data. |
| **Smartphone** | A multifunctional device for communication and other needs. |
| **Digital Camera** | Captures moments as images and videos. |
| **Portable Media Player** | Listen to music and watch videos anywhere. |
| **E-book Reader** | Read books in digital format. |
| **Gaming Device** | Consoles or dedicated computers for playing games. |
